Loteria

Loteria is like bingo but a little more interactive. It’s a classic Mexican game that has images instead of numbers on the boards. It’s also a fun way to pick up some words in Spanish.

Instructions

  • Select a Board
  • When a card is dealt, look for the same image in your board
  • Click on your board, where you have the same image
  • If you get 3 images in a row, you win!
  • Click the Loteria button
  • You lose if you miss 2 cards dealt
